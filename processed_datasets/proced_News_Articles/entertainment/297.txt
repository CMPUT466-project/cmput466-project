film comedy meet the fockers sequel to meet the parents has topped the us box office for a third week
the movie which stars ben stiller robert de niro dustin hoffman and barbra streisand took 285m £152m making a total of 2043m £109m
michael keaton thriller white noise was second with 24m £128m despite negative reviews
martin scorseses howard hughes biopic the aviator came third with 76m £4m and a total of 429m £228m
childrens adventure lemony snickets a series of unfortunate events starring jim carrey and meryl streep fell two places to fourth position with 74m £39m
it is also the ninth jim carrey film to hit the 100m £532m mark according to exhibitor relations
and fat albert a liveaction adaptation of bill cosbys tv cartoon fell one place to number five with takings over the three days beginning on friday of 5m £26m
oceans twelve took sixth place in the us chart with 54m £28m but it led the nonus chart at the weekend according to hollywood reporter with an estimated 177m £94m and total takings of 137m £73m
the incredibles took 300m £160m outside the us while meet the fockers topped box office charts in australia and mexico with nonus takings of 1935m £102m
